Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour through our website, by calling us, or by visiting our office. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for the best availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major credit cards. A deposit is required to confirm your booking.

Do you offer custom tours?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom tours tailored to your preferences. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days before the tour date are fully refundable. Cancellations made 3-7 days before receive a 50% refund.

About Musafir Kahani

Your local guides to the heart of Pakistan, based in Lahore

Our Story

Musafir Kahani was born from the dreams of two friends, Rizwan Naveed and Talha Bakht, both proud Lahoris with a deep love for Pakistan's landscapes and culture. We grew up exploring the vibrant streets of Lahore and the breathtaking valleys of the north, always eager to share the real Pakistan with fellow travelers.

We believe that the best journeys are those that connect you with local people, traditions, and stories. That's why every Musafir Kahani tour is designed and led by us—locals who know the hidden gems, the best food spots, and the true spirit of our homeland.

We're not a big company. We're two passionate locals, committed to giving you an authentic, safe, and memorable experience—whether you're wandering the old city of Lahore or trekking in the mountains. Join us, and let's write your travel story together!
